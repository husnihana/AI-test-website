Interacting with Chat AI

Chat AI allows users to interact directly with documents using natural language. Instead of reading the entire document, you can simply ask questions.

How to Use Chat AI

Step 1 — Navigate to Document

Navigate to the document you want to analyze and select it from your Category or My Box.

Step 2 — Open AI Search

Click AI Search to open the Chat AI window for that document.

Step 3 — Ask a Question

Enter your question in plain language. Examples: Summarize this document, What is the total quotation value?, List all suppliers mentioned.

Step 4 — Get Results

The AI analyzes the document and returns accurate results instantly. No reading required.

Example Queries

  • Summarize this document
  • What is the total quotation value?
  • List all suppliers mentioned
  • Extract invoice numbers
  • What decisions were made in this meeting?
