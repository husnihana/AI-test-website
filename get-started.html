What is Doc-Scribe.ai

Doc-Scribe.ai is an AI-powered document intelligence platform that helps organizations manage, understand, and extract knowledge from their documents.

The Problem We Solve

Too Many Documents

Modern organizations generate thousands of reports, policies, contracts, and project records that contain valuable but hard-to-reach knowledge.

Slow Manual Search

Finding the right information requires employees to manually search files and read large volumes of content — a slow and inefficient process.

Ask Your Documents

Doc-Scribe.ai lets you ask questions directly to your documents using conversational AI, retrieving accurate answers in seconds.

Instant AI Insights

The system reads your documents, understands their content, and delivers summaries and key insights through a chat-based interface.

In Simple Terms

  • You no longer search your documents — you ask them
  • Store and organize documents securely
  • Search documents using natural language
  • Analyze documents with AI
  • Extract key information automatically
  • Generate summaries and insights instantly
