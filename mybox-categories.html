My Box vs Categories

Doc-Scribe.ai provides two document storage environments to suit personal and shared team workflows.

Storage Options

My Box

Your personal document space. Files stored here are private by default and visible only to you. Ideal for temporary files, drafts, and personal research.

Categories

Shared departmental folders. Documents stored in categories can be accessed by other users within the same department. Ideal for team collaboration.

My Box — Use Cases

Personal research, draft documents, and private notes that are not ready to be shared with your team.

Categories — Example

Sales Department categories may include: Quotations, Price Book, Competitor Information, and Tenders — all shared across the team.

Key Differences

  • My Box is private — only visible to you
  • Categories are shared within a department
  • Use My Box for drafts and personal files
  • Use Categories for team collaboration
