Upload Your First Document

Uploading a document is the first step to using Doc-Scribe.ai. Follow the steps below to get started.

Upload Steps

Step 1 — Click Upload

Click Upload from the navigation menu to begin the upload process.

Step 2 — Choose Destination

Select My Box for private storage or a Category for departmental shared storage.

Steps 3–5 — Select & Stage

Select a file from your computer. It moves to Staged Files. Select the uploaded file and confirm the file category.

Step 6 — Upload Now

Click Upload Now. The system automatically processes, extracts text, indexes content, and makes the document available for AI search.

After Uploading

  • The document is processed automatically
  • Text is extracted and indexed
  • Document becomes available for AI search
  • You can interact with it via Chat AI
