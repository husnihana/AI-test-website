Using Collections

Collections allow you to group documents from different categories into a single virtual workspace. Original documents remain in their categories.

Working with Collections

Create a Collection

Click Collections → New Collection, enter a collection name, then click Create.

Add Documents

Navigate to a Category, tick the document, click the Collections icon, select an existing collection, then click Add.

Example Use Case

Collection: Client Submissions — pulling documents from Quotations, Price Book, Competitor Information, and Tender Documents into one view.

Delete a Collection

Click Collections, tick the collection, click the Delete icon, type Delete, and confirm. This removes the collection, not the original documents.

Key Points

  • Collections are virtual — original documents stay in their categories
  • Group documents from multiple categories for a project or case
  • Deleting a collection does not delete the original documents
  • Navigate to Collections to view all your grouped documents
