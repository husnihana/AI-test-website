Understanding AI Modes

Doc-Scribe provides six AI modes designed for different types of document analysis. Choose the mode that best fits your task.

The Six AI Modes

General Query

Used for everyday questions and summaries. Ask anything about your document in plain language. Example: Summarize this document.

Visualizer Mode

Creates charts, diagrams, and visual summaries from document data. Useful for reports, presentations, and data analysis.

Analyst Mode

Performs deeper analysis and identifies patterns or inconsistencies. Example: Compare supplier quotations.

Reviewer Mode

Checks documents for errors, missing information, or inconsistencies. Example: Check if there are calculation errors.

Transcript Mode

Analyzes meeting transcripts and extracts key discussions, decisions made, and tasks assigned from the document.

Extractor Mode

Extracts structured data from documents such as invoices or reports — Invoice Number, Supplier, Item details, Total amount — exportable to ERP or Excel.

Choosing the Right Mode

  • Use General Query for quick summaries and everyday questions
  • Use Visualizer for reports and data presentations
  • Use Analyst to compare or find patterns across documents
  • Use Reviewer to check for errors or missing data
  • Use Transcript for meeting notes and action items
  • Use Extractor to pull structured data for ERP or Excel export
