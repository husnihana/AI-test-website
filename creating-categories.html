Creating Categories

Categories help organize documents within departments, making it easy for teams to find and collaborate on shared files.

How to Create a Category

Step 1 — Manage Categories

Click Manage Categories from the navigation menu and ensure the Main Tab is selected.

Step 2 — Enter a Name

Enter the Category Name. For example: Quotations, Price Book, or Tenders.

Step 3 — Customize (Optional)

Choose an icon and color to make the category visually distinct and easy to identify in the list.

Step 4 — Create

Click Create. The category will appear in the category list and is ready to store documents.

Good to Know

  • Categories help structure documents for collaboration
  • Each department can have multiple categories
  • Documents in categories are accessible to department members
  • Icons and colors are optional but help with navigation
